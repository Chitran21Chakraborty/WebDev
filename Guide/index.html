Top three activities to do in Jaipur

Iconic Honeycomb Facade - HawaMahal

This unique pink sandstone marvel features 953 intricately carved windows designed to let cool breezes flow through.

Majestic Hilltop Fortress- Amber Fort

A stunning fusion of Hindu and Mughal architecture featuring the glittering Sheesh Mahal and panoramic lake views.

Opulent Royal Complex- City Palace

A magnificent blend of Rajput and Mughal styles featuring ornate gates, sprawling courtyards, and the royal family's residence.
